About Us

Based in India, Loyal Pythonic Food is a family owned Indian restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from South Indian and North Indian culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Loyal Pythonic Food is owned by two Indian brothers, Nrupen and Samir, who moved to the Other States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Samir relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Hyderabad.

Nrupen does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Indian to incorporate additional cuisines from the mediterranean region.
